Flora and Milo: Page 3

After walking through the forest for a while, Flora and Milo came up upon the daisies they were looking for. However, there was only one.

Flora and Milo decide to get the flower anyways. They agree that they will decide who will get the daisy later. So as they reach for it...

...the flower is suddenly taken! In a whoosh of a black shadow, a cat runs by, snatching the flower before Milo or Flora has the chance to realize what has happened.
